How to Pay?

Simply make your payment via internet and mobile banking and enjoy automatic bill payment update with Dot Internet's automatic billing system. Here is the step by step procedure on how to pay online.

Get connection Now

Pay Bill through bKash

Login to your bKash account with your account's PIN number and tap on "পে বিল" option.

In " পে বিল " option you'll find "South net". Simply tap on it and follow the next step.

Now select your internet billing timeline in " বিল সময়সীমা " box & input your customer in " বিল একাউন্ট নম্বর দিন " box.

If you want to save the payment info for quick payment, you can simple tick the checkbox " ভবিষ্যৎ বিল পেমেন্টের জন্য একাউন্টটি সেভ করে রাখুন ".

After that, tap the " পে বিল করতে এগিয়ে যান " button & hold the pay button for a few second. You're done!

Pay Bill through Nagad

Login to your Nagad account with your account's PIN number and tap on "পে বিল" option.

In " পে বিল " option you'll find "South net". Simply tap on it and follow the next step.

Now enter your USER ID in " কন্টাক্ট নং" box & input your phone number in " কাস্টমার নাম্বার " box.

After that, tap the " পরবর্তী" button & proceeds the payment. You're done!
